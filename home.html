TALK TO US

Light is being developed by Team Member in VietNam. We're a team of 4 people who, alongside making the game, are responsible for building these websites, cutting those game trailers, posting regular game updates, answering questions on social media and much more. Though it's a lot of work, we love doing it and it's made even more enjoyable by the enthusiastic support we've received from fans this whole way.
